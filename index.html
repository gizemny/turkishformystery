Andrew Harper

  • Category: UX/UI Design, Web Design, Graphic Design, Marketing

Andrew Harper is a luxury travel publication and full-service travel agency. I am a web designer in a small web team and together we are making great strides in growing the company's web presence. Below are some examples of the work I have done.

There have been many versions, wireframes, mockups, meetings and user-testing sessions involved.
If you are interested in learning more about any of these projects, please contact me.

I was the lead designer for the Auctions redesign. The old auctions system had an outdated design cluttered with content. My goal was to let these amazing auction deals breathe with an attractive responsive design which would allow the users to easily locate information and take action. Also in the scope of this project, I designed the auctions profile/on-boarding page and an instructions page. The new design includes live bidding, 'buy now' functionality and a countdown timer.

Since the redesign, we have seen significant increase in auction user registration, engagement and sales.
